"Unlocking Career Insights in Data Professions: A Global Survey Analysis"

In this project, we delve into a rich dataset encompassing responses from 630 professionals spanning data science, data analysis, data engineering, and related fields. Collected through an online survey, our objective is to unveil insights into the experiences of these professionals.

Survey Scope and Cleaning:
Our survey reached individuals across diverse regions, including the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, and more. We aim to understand the challenges they faced entering their job markets, assess their job satisfaction, and explore their work-life balance. We also investigate the educational and experiential backgrounds that facilitated their career transitions.. The data is organized into three tables: Orders, Customers, and Products, with primary or secondary keys linking these tables, following a relational database structure similar to SQL databases.

Data Enhancement and Analysis
The data underwent a meticulous cleaning process to ensure accuracy and uniformity. Using Power Query, we standardized and cleaned entries, replacing values when necessary. Delimiters were employed to split certain columns, such as city from state or first and last names, making the data more accessible for analysis. Additionally, open-ended questions under 'others' were standardized using delimiters and any unwanted or erroneous data were removed.

Interactive Visualizations with Power BI:
To bring the data to life, we utilize Power BI. Its dynamic capabilities allow users to interact with the data, exploring regional disparities and specific job roles. Users can customize variables and graphs in real-time, focusing on countries, job titles, or programming languages.

Insights Revealed
The resulting visualizations provide insights into salary satisfaction, work-life balance, and career entry challenges. This project delivers a comprehensive analysis of data professions, offering a robust platform for understanding these dynamic career paths.
